Intel Quartus Prime .bdf File to Vector Graphic Converter

A simple open-source client-side converter for Intel Quartus Prime .bdf schematics to vector graphics. Written solely in JavaScript. Developed by Lukas Zrout, HTBL Hollabrunn 2020.
